Event
===
(PK)
- event_id: INT64
(One-to-one relationships)
- sleep_id: INT64
- food_id: INT64
- symptom_group_id: INT64
(Attributes)
- type: ENUM
    - SLEEP
    - SPORT
    - SYMPTOM_GROUP
    - FOOD
- creation_time: DATETIME

FOOD
===
(PK)
- food_id: INT64
(Many-to-one relationships)
- recipe_id: INT64
(Attributes)
- ingredients: JSONField

RECIPE
===
(PK)
- recipe_id: INT64
# (One-to-many relationships)
# - food_id: INT64
(Attributes)
- name: CharField
- ingredients: JSONField
